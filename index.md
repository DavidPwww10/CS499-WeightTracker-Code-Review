## Why I Chose This Artifact

I selected the Weight Tracker artifact because it gives me a strong opportunity to demonstrate growth across multiple areas of computer science within a single project. It supports discussion of software design, data handling, maintainability, usability, and professional presentation.

Using the Weight Tracker application also allowed me to show how one project can be improved from several professional perspectives. I was able to focus on the structure of the application, how information is handled, how the user experience can be improved, and how the final work can be presented clearly for reviewers and future employers.
